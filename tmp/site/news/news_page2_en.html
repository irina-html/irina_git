Company's news
03.03.2016

It began production of halal confectionery products under the trademark of "good smack"

Kharkov Plant "Foodstuffs" - modern and stable enterprise on production and sale of confectionery products.

The factory was established back in 1966 and since then is one of the leading industrial enterprises of Ukraine.

In the development of the company its name and legal form changed, while the level of product quality remained high, as production volumes increase every year.

Currently, the Kharkov factory "Foodstuffs" specializes in the production of confectionery products like butter and biscuits, wafers, chocolate wafer and sufleynye candy delight. The product range is constantly expanding and improving in order to be able to meet the needs and wishes of our customers and end-users.

The company uses advanced confectionery manufacturing technology, as well as to attract the best specialists for quality control, as the products and incoming ingredients used in the manufacturing process.

For high quality products, increase its competitiveness, expanding the range to meet the needs of the population Kharkov factory "Foodstuffs" was repeatedly awarded diplomas of the International open rating of popularity and quality of goods and services "Golden Fortune" International exhibition-fair "Agro-2004" National Exhibition -yarmarki "Products of Ukraine", diplomas of the Ministry of agrarian policy of Ukraine. In 2008 the company was awarded a gold medal for the best product in the category "for the production of high quality food products"..
