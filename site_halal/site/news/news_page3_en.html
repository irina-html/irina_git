Halal Ice Cream TM Laska

Enterprise of public association "Association of support domestic producers", "Laska Company" was founded in 2000 on the territory of the former MASLOSYRBAZA.

Production capacity of the enterprise is one of the largest in the former USSR. Since they are high-quality refrigeration equipment of domestic and foreign production. The company is equipped with modern equipment according to European standard, it high-class specialists.

Industrial and technological laboratory monitors the entire process from raw material to finished product. It has two branches: physical, chemical and microbiological tests. Laboratory itself and its experts, in turn, are verified by relevant organizations.

The products are manufactured exclusively from natural ingredients: non-condensed and skimmed milk powder, butter and sugar. Ice cream brand "Lusk" is manufactured using advanced technology.

Implementation of new kinds of ice cream - it is now more than a hundred species.

"Firm Laska" - this has long been not just a factory for the production of ice cream. It is a complex structure, which operates a factory of frozen convenience foods. On the Ukrainian market of products of this company represented trademarks "Weasel", "generous", "My grandmother." Recently, "Weasel" has entered the market with TM "Sonechko" "Dad's snack", "League of taste" and "Shіdnіlasoschі".

Today, the company "weasel", member of the Association "Ukrainian Ice Cream" and is one of the leaders of Ukrainian ice cream industry, as well as one of the five leaders of the Ukrainian market of ice cream.
