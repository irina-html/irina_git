Ukrainian beef with a "Halal" by LLC "Meats IPC"

LLC "Meats IPC" manufactures and distributes beef. Through the use of new efficient meat production technology, production of "Meats IPC" it has high quality and complies with all relevant regulations. The company successfully works in the domestic market, and exports its products to the CIS countries and the Middle East, received the certificate from the Halal Research Centre and Halal Certification "Alraid" in 2016.

LLC "Meats IPC" guarantees:

- high quality meat;

- affordable prices;

- assortment beef processing;

- a full package of accompanying documents;

- the possibility of individual orders, as well as the manufacture of goods «Private label»

For beef cattle production company harvests, which is grown in the territory of Western Ukraine. The company is located in an ecologically clean region. Slaughter produced industrially by the modern equipment of its own slaughterhouse. Depending on the need for the line allows the company to obtain products in both halves chilled and deep-frozen. As well as meat deep-frozen in blocks, offal and skin.

The professionalism of the team, constant and uninterrupted supply of high-quality meat products, as well as the optimal level of prices ensured the confidence of, both consumers and business partners.
