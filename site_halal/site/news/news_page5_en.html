Participation Halal Certification Center "Al-Raid" at the conference ContiTech- Ukraine

February 16-17, 2016 at "Puscha Voditsa" held a conference «CondiTeh-Ukraine" with the assistance of the Center for the study and certification of halal "Alraid".

«CondiTech-Ukraine "- a two-day conference for members of the confectionery and bakery market of Ukraine. The range of topics included issues of concern to everyone today head of the company - from the expansion of the marketing and research of export opportunities to the industrial and technological challenges.

During the event, participants had the opportunity to learn more about what is happening on the finished products and raw materials markets, find new contacts and opportunities for export, to get a "baggage" of ideas to develop your own business at all its stages - from production to sale.

The conference discussed a number of important issues for domestic producers, among them:

- market of confectionery and bakery products 2015-2016 - Trends and Prospects;

- export of confectionery products - an overview of promising markets and output algorithms;

- marketing and sales strategy, effective solutions and working scheme
